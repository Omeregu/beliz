About Us

1. We focus on the education and resettlement of abused and deprived children. 2. Support young female entrepreneurs through capacity building and networking.
3.To educate, protect, care and Assist Needy Girls, women and Children in Deprived Rural Communities through Education, Work in Partnership with Individuals, NGOs and Institutions around The World to end Poverty and Social Issues that affect Girls, Women and Children Particularly in Rural Communities.

Our Mission

Our goal is to invest in sustainable solutions that will mitigate or end poverty, provide equal opportunities for social, economic and educational development, and that will ensure a life free from violence.

To work tirelessly to ensure that women have adequate representation access to social welfare, education, and healthcare, empowering women from all backgrounds. women pave the path to progress by providing access to quality education and women's entrepreneurship platforms.
